classic pancakes

According to tradition, the dough is made with milk. But the main secret of the basis of this dish is that the dry ingredients are mixed separately from the liquid ones and then combined with the latter. To make milk pancakes fragrant, add a pinch of vanilla or vanilla sugar to the dough.

components

  • 1 Art. milk;
  • 1 egg;
  • 1 tsp. baking powder;
  • 1.5 tbsp. wheat flour;
  • 3 Art. l. olive oil;
  • 3 Art. l. sugar;
  • vanilla, salt - to taste.

Cooking time – 15–20 minutes.

The number of servings is 3–4.

Step-by-step instructions

  1. Pour milk, olive oil into the mixer, add|add| egg. Beat the products at high speed until white foam is formed.
  2. In a deep bowl, combine the dry bulk ingredients.
  3. Add to the mixer, mix with the milk-egg mass at high speeds. Leave for 5 minutes.
  4. Heat a frying pan (preferably with a non-stick coating) over medium heat without greasing.
  5. Pour the batter onto the hot surface to form a pancake.
  6. When each pancake is browned on the bottom, pull it up with a spatula, turn it over and bake it on the other side.
